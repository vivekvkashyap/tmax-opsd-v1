Fix the Makefile's flags so it builds cleanly with `-Wall -Wextra` and no warnings. 854x480 RGB24 is exactly `854*480*3` bytes per frame — extract that with `ffmpeg ... -pix_fmt rgb24 -vframes 1 -f rawvideo`.
