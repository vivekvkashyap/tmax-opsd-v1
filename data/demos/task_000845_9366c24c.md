Treat frequency extraction and drift extraction as two separate, standard signal-processing steps rather than one combined fit — mixing them (e.g. fitting a line before removing periodicity, or vice versa) is where naive attempts go wrong. Load the CSV with pandas/numpy; for the dominant frequency, run `np.fft.rfft` (or full `fft`) on the `latency_us` column, compute the corresponding frequency axis with `np.fft.rfftfreq(n, d=sampling_interval_seconds)` — remember timestamps are in milliseconds and 1 sample/ms, so the sampling interval in seconds is 0.001 and Nyquist is 500 Hz — take magnitudes, explicitly exclude/zero the 0 Hz (DC) bin, then find the frequency at the magnitude argmax and round to the nearest integer Hz.
