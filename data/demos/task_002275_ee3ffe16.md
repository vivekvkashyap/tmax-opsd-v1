Phase 1: connect to `/home/user/ecommerce.db` with `sqlite3`, and issue all queries with `?` placeholders (parameterized), never string-formatted SQL, even for static queries — construct a `networkx.MultiDiGraph` (or plain DiGraph) adding `User`/`Product` nodes and `PURCHASED` edges from purchases, `REVIEWED` edges (storing `rating` as edge data) from reviews. Phase 3: for each target product, look up its category and title from the products table/graph node data, group into a dict of category -> sorted list of titles, and `json.dump` to `/home/user/insights.json` with sorted keys for determinism.
