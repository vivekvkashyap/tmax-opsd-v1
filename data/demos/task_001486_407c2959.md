Do all validation before trusting any file content, and never `cd` into or follow a symlink while validating. Only after the archive passes every check should you locate `app_config.json` files (`find . -name app_config.json`) and convert each with a small `jq` or Python one-liner producing `<config><key>value</key>...</config>` (order fields as they appear in the JSON), writing `app_config.xml` alongside and removing the `.json`. Repack with `tar -czf` into the output dir as `sanitized_bundle.tar.gz`, then print ACCEPTED/exit 0 or REJECTED/exit 1 as appropriate.
