This is a known-plaintext attack on a byte-wise XOR stream cipher driven by an LCG (X_{n+1} = a*X_n + c mod 256, C_i = P_i XOR X_i). Read `crypto_module.py` to confirm the exact keystream recurrence, then hex-decode `suspicious_payload.hex` into ciphertext bytes. Since you know the plaintext is Base64 of `{"cmd":"audit"...`, compute the known Base64 prefix bytes yourself, XOR them against the first ciphertext bytes to recover the first several keystream bytes X_0, X_1, X_2, X_3. Once (seed, a, c) are confirmed by regenerating the keystream and matching more ciphertext bytes, decrypt the full payload, Base64-decode it, and json.loads it to get cmd/dir/vuln.
