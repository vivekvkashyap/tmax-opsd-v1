Build prep_config.sh as a chain of sed operations: drop lines matching leading-whitespace-then-`#` (`/^[[:space:]]*#/d`), drop blank/whitespace-only lines (`/^[[:space:]]*$/d`), collapse runs of internal whitespace to a single space (`s/[[:space:]]+/ /g`), and trim leading/trailing whitespace — pipe these in sequence into clean_config.txt. In build_manifest.c, parse the INCLUDE/EXCLUDE lines into simple arrays, then recursively walk each INCLUDE directory (opendir/readdir or nftw), using `lstat` — not `stat` — on every entry so symlinks are detected and skipped outright, since following symlinks with `stat` is exactly what caused the original infinite loop. Skip files whose name ends in any EXCLUDE extension, and for every remaining regular file, `popen("sha256sum <path>", "r")` and parse the hash token out of its output. Write "hash path" lines and sort the final output (qsort in C, or shell out to `sort`) before saving manifest.txt.
