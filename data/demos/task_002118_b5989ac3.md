Take `math.Abs` of each element, sum, and divide each by the sum to normalize into a probability vector. Watch the sign ambiguity of SVD: since you take the absolute value before normalizing, the sign of gonum's output doesn't matter for P, but double check by re-deriving on a small example that the resulting distribution is sensible (sums to 1, all non-negative).
