For each of the 4 files, launch a goroutine (coordinate completion with a `sync.WaitGroup` or channel) that opens the FITS file, iterates HDUs to reach the binary table extension, and reads the `FLUX` column into a `[]float64` of length 1024. Feed that slice to `fft.FFTReal` (or equivalent), then take `cmplx.Abs` of each complex output to get magnitudes. A common mistake is searching the full spectrum for the peak — the spec requires restricting the search to indices 1..512 inclusive (excluding the DC term at index 0) when finding the dominant-frequency index k. Solve `3.5x - 12.0 = k` algebraically for x to get CalibratedFreq. For MeanMagnitude, average magnitudes over indices 1..1023 (still excluding index 0, but here covering the whole array, not just up to 512 — don't reuse the same range as the peak search). Marshal results per filename into the required JSON shape and write to `/home/user/results.json`.
