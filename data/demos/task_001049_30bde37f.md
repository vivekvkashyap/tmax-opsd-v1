Normalize: after all patients are resampled and concatenated, compute one global min/max for each of `heart_rate` and `temperature` across the whole interpolated dataset, then min-max scale every value into [0,1] using those two global scalars — normalizing per-patient instead of globally is a common mistake here. Finally sort by anonymized_id then timestamp and write the four required columns.
