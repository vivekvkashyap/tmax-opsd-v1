For the Wasserstein step, feed the tight-tolerance (Run B) simulated concentrations and the sorted observed concentrations (in the same time order) into `scipy.stats.wasserstein_distance` as two 1D samples. Write both floats to analysis_results.json under the exact keys given. Sanity check your numbers by eyeballing whether the max_stability_diff is small (both solvers should roughly agree on this smooth exponential decay) and whether the Wasserstein distance is a small positive number reflecting observation noise around the true decay curve.
