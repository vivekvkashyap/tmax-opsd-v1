Third, after finding the best `(p0, p1)` by minimum MSE, re-simulate with those parameters, collect the residuals (`obs - model`) across all particles/timesteps, and compute their standard deviation (mean, then sqrt of mean squared deviation) — do this as a second pass after grid search, not incrementally during it. Compile with `-lm`, run, and format the three values to exactly 4 decimal places, comma-separated, in solution.txt.
