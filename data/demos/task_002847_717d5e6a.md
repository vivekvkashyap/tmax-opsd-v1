The race condition is a classic OpenMP shared-accumulator bug: multiple threads incrementing `global_sum` inside `#pragma omp parallel for` without synchronization. Fix it by adding `reduction(+:global_sum)` to the pragma clause (i.e. `#pragma omp parallel for reduction(+:global_sum)`) — this gives each thread a private partial sum that OpenMP combines safely at the end, rather than manually adding locks/atomics or wrapping the whole loop in a critical section, which would kill performance and isn't what's being asked. Do not touch the signal-generation math, noise formula, or N — only the pragma line.
