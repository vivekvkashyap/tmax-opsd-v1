(1) Load: glob `readings_*.jsonl`, parse each line with `serde_json`, and read `metadata.csv` with the `csv` crate into a `HashMap<meter_id, region_id>`. (2) Dedup: build a `HashMap<(meter_id, timestamp), reading_kw>` and on collision keep `f64::max` of the two values — do this in parallel-safe fashion (e.g. `rayon` par-iter into per-chunk maps, then merge) rather than a naive linear scan if you want real parallelism. (3) Join/filter: drop any reading whose meter_id isn't in the metadata map. (4) Per-meter Z-score: group readings by meter_id, compute mean and *population* std dev (divide by N, not N-1 — verify against the worked example in the task, e.g. two readings 12 and 14 giving std=1.0, which is population, not sample), and set z=0.0 when std==0 or count==1. (5) Truncate each timestamp to the top of the hour (zero out minutes/seconds) using `chrono`, group by (region_id, hour), sum z-scores. (6) Sort output by region_id then hour ascending, format the sum to exactly 4 decimals, and write CSV with the exact header `region_id,hour,sum_z_score` to `/home/user/output/regional_load.csv`.
