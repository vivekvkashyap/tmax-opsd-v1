Start by reading `/app/vendored/goproxy-1.0/main.go` to locate the goroutine/channel dispatch logic — the deadlock pattern to look for is an unbuffered channel where a worker goroutine blocks writing a result back with no concurrent reader draining it (or a dispatch channel with no buffer and mismatched producer/consumer counts).
