The Robertson system is famously stiff because of the huge `3e7` and `1e4` rate constants, so an explicit RK method will need vanishingly small steps and diverge/blow up with any reasonable step size — that's the bug in the prior attempt. Use an adaptive or conservatively small fixed step and iterate until you reach `t_end`.
