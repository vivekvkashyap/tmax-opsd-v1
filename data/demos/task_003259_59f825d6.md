Phase 3: build `/home/user/log_classifier.py` iteratively — decode URL-encoding and strip whitespace/nested-tag obfuscation before pattern-matching for SQLi/XSS signatures, and continually test against both decrypted corpora until zero false positives/negatives remain, since a naive regex will overfit or miss obfuscated evil samples.
