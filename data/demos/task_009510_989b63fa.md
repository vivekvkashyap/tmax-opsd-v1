Verify with `readelf -d main | grep -i rpath` that the rpath actually landed in the binary, and test by running `main` from a clean shell (`env -i PATH=$PATH ./main` or similar) rather than one where you've already exported LD_LIBRARY_PATH, since that would mask a broken rpath.
